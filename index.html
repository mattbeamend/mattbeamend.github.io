Privacy Policy - Capital Compass

This Privacy Policy outlines how Capital Compass ("we", "us", or "our") handles information collected from users of the Capital Compass application.

Information Collection and Use

Capital Compass allows users to track their investment deposits. It does not collect any personally identifiable information from its users. All data, including investment transactions, account balances, and financial metrics, is saved locally on the user's device and is not accessible or saved onto any external machine.

Log Data

We do not log any data externally. Your investment activity within the Capital Compass app is stored exclusively on your device for your personal use.

Cookies

The Capital Compass application does not use cookies. We prioritize user privacy by avoiding the use of tracking technologies.

Security

Security is a top priority. All investment data is stored locally on the user's device, and no information is transmitted or saved externally. While we employ commercially acceptable measures to protect your information, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure. We continuously work to enhance and adapt our security protocols to meet evolving challenges.

Contact Us & Support

If you have any questions, concerns, or need support regarding this Privacy Policy or the Capital Compass application, please contact us at matt@beamend.com. We value your feedback and are committed to addressing your inquiries promptly.
